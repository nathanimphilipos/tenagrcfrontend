Welcome to TenaGRC

Making GRC Simple

In today’s fast-moving world, staying on top of cybersecurity and compliance can feel overwhelming, especially when you’re juggling growth, customers, and product deadlines. That’s why I built TenaGRC. It’s a simpler way to make sense of your organization’s risk without needing to dig through frameworks or hire a full-blown GRC team. With our Risk Analysis Tool, you can get an instant, automated risk visualization tailored to your environment. Behind the scenes, it maps key inputs, like how often you test vulnerabilities or the security training when onboarding new employees. We ask what actually matters. But we don’t stop at the visuals. TenaGRC is built to walk with you from Risk insight to Control implementation.

If you’re a startup or small company without the budget for a GRC consultant, this is for you. We help you create a functioning GRC foundation for your business. Writing policies, mapping controls, preparing for audits, and ensuring security without the enterprise price tag. I'm a college student who’s worked in cybersecurity and compliance. I know what’s needed, and I built TenaGRC to deliver it in a way that makes sense, saves time, and keeps you moving forward.

About Us

TenaGRC was built on a dream that started in Ethiopia, the beautiful scenery at the top of this page. Ethiopia was a land where creativity outpaced resources, and imagination was often the greatest tool I had.

I’ve always been fascinated by the way technology can solve real-world problems. As I studied cybersecurity and gained experience through internships, I discovered a passion for helping organizations make sense of risk. But I also noticed a gap. Too many companies were overwhelmed by confusing frameworks and technical jargon.

I wanted to change that.

My name is Nathanim Philipos, and I founded TenaGRC to make risk simple, clear, and accessible. "Tena" means health in Amharic, my native tongue. Just like you check your physical health with a quick scan or test, I believe organizations should be able to check their risk health without going through long, exhausting readiness assessments or non-technical audits.

TenaGRC is a vision, to take the stress and confusion out of Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), and replace it with clarity, automation, and confidence. Our goal is to empower leaders and teams to see where they stand, understand what matters, and act wisely—no technical jargon required.

This is just the beginning. One day, I hope TenaGRC becomes a tool that helps revolutionize how audits are done: faster, smarter, and more human.
